OpenAudioMc is super simple to use

Seriously! players can have it all setup in just 10 seconds after joining your server for the first time!

STEP 1

The player joins your minecraft server with their preferred version and client

STEP 2

The player gets a message asking them to connect when joining or after running /audio, simply click on the chat message

STEP 3

The player has to confirm that they want to open the link

STEP 4

Click "activate session" in the newly opened web page

FINISH

Congrats! You're now connected! You can now head back to the game and enjoy the ambient music and sound effects, or dive deeper and activate Proximity Voice Chat
